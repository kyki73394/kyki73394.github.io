CURIS
2021-2022

This student organization dedicates themselves to avocating for improved public health and health education.

Designing the Home Page

I created a wireframe for the home page in a slideshow to be shared with my team. I kept in mind the color scheme of the logo as well as the cleanliness of the inspiration websites that CURIS had provided. From there, I was able to construct a page with minimalistic buttons and simple navigation as a gateway to the rest of the site.

In Development

I hadn't used React before this project, so I was interested to see how it would function differently from other coding languages. At first it was difficult to catch onto the structure of the functions and the use of constant variables . . . constantly, but I do find the use of separate componenets to be very helpful (for organization, debugging, and readability).

Uncover
2022

This student organization interviews and sharews narratives from Asians and Asian-Americans across campus and small-businesses.

Dalgona Flyer

Drawn to encourage fans of the Netflix show Squid Game to buy cookies and learn more about the organization.

T-Shirt Design

I wanted to capture the word "uncover" in a drawing while also drawing parallels to the purpose behind the organization. I played around with many iterations of peeling back the layers of a phone screen (as the narratives collected by the org are shared on social media), and I chose this design for its clarity and interesting perspective.
